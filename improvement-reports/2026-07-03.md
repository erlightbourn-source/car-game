**The finding that drove this run:** economy pacing. A young player earns ~214 coins/run and could afford the priciest item in any category in ≤ 4.2 runs; a tween/teen (~1,050 coins/run) could buy the entire catalog in about 2 runs. After that, coins are meaningless — no reason to grab another one.

## Research findings

1. **Unlock pacing is the retention lever in this genre.** Subway Surfers' meta loop is daily/weekly missions plus characters and boards priced far above a single session's earnings; Crossy Road's coin-priced character catalog is the canonical "no purchase needed, but always something to save for" design ([Carry1st on Subway Surfers 2025](https://www.carry1st.com/blog/subway-surfers-in-2025-new-updates-and-features-for-the-iconic-endless-runner), [Crossy Road](https://www.crossyroad.com/updates/subwaysurferscrossyroad), [EJAW endless-runner design guide](https://ejaw.net/what-is-an-endless-runner-game/)). Implication: Lane Rush needed a price tier that takes multiple sessions to reach.
2. **Short-term goal + long-term goal, always both.** Genre guidance is a mission/daily layer for today's session plus aspirational unlocks for the week ([Game-Ace endless-runner guide](https://game-ace.com/blog/how-to-develop-an-endless-runner-game-in-unreal-engine/), [Pixelfield retention design](https://pixelfield.co.uk/blog/how-to-design-games-for-retention/)). Lane Rush already has the short loop (Daily Challenge, streak, missions); the long loop was the gap.
3. **First-week retention carries the game** — most lifetime engagement comes from players who survive week one ([RoLearn first-week retention](https://rolearn.dev/guidance/first-week-retention-optimization/)); a week of play should not exhaust the catalog. At ~226 coins/run the new top item ≈ 7 runs — about a week of casual sessions.
4. **Hybrid monetization (rewarded ads + IAP) dominates the genre's top-grossing list** ([Marketwillion](https://marketwillion.com/how-to-develop-fast-paced-endless-runner-like-subway-surfers/)) — noted for completeness and expressly NOT implemented; monetization is a standing flag-only item for this game.
