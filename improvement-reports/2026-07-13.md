## Research grounding
- Prior venture intel (Ada, 2026-07-05, Game Growth Advisor / Tenjin) framed the win condition for kid endless-runners as **hybrid-casual light meta-progression that pulls players back past D1/D7** — legible next goals, not deeper punishment. Read against Lane Rush: the coin-priced cosmetic ladder is the retention lever, and its top rung (Volcano 650🪙 ≈ 3.3 runs) had become quickly reachable, flattening the "next goal" pull for engaged players. Adding a more-distant, visually distinct ceiling directly serves that retention mechanic without touching difficulty or fairness.
- Space/cosmos themes are a proven high-appeal, kid-safe cosmetic motif across the genre (Subway Surfers seasonal worlds, Crossy Road unlockables) — a good fit for a fresh premium ceiling that reads as aspirational.
