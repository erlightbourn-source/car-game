## Research findings

1. **Near-miss reward feedback drives engagement and replay.** Burnout's "Near Miss" — passing
   extremely close to another vehicle at high speed without contact — is the same mechanic Lane
   Rush rewards. Near-miss/almost-win feedback engages reward pathways and extends sessions; this
   is exactly what Lane Rush's combo system models. Implication: the combo achievement deserves
   to be *visible* as a result, not just a transient in-run popup.
   Sources: [Burnout Wiki — Near Miss](https://burnout.fandom.com/wiki/Near_Miss),
   [Psychology of Games — The Near Miss Effect](https://www.psychologyofgames.com/2016/09/the-near-miss-effect-and-game-rewards/)
2. **Runners retain better when they give clear goals beyond raw score.** Endless runners have
   low baseline retention because difficulty ramps fast; the fix is clear objectives and
   status/competition signals (leaderboards, streaks) layered on top of scoring. Implication: a
   "best combo this run" stat on the game-over screen is a cheap, skill-based beat-your-streak
   goal that complements the existing leaderboard.
   Source: [Endless Runner Games — Core Mechanics and Top Titles](https://logicsimplified.com/newgames/endless-runner-games-core-mechanics-and-top-titles/)

## Research findings

1. **Streak + milestone systems are the dominant retention lever for runners.** Apps combining
   streaks and milestones see 40–60% higher DAU and cut 30-day churn ~35% vs. single-feature
   implementations; a 7+ day streak makes users 2.3× more likely to engage daily.
   *Implication:* Lane Rush already has a 7-day daily-streak calendar (`shop.js` `claimDailyBonus`/
   `streakInfo`) — the bones are strong; the cheap next win is making the current streak visible
   beyond the load-time toast.
   Source: [Streaks and Milestones for Gamification — Plotline](https://www.plotline.so/blog/streaks-for-gamification-in-mobile-apps)
2. **Unlockable content / customization sustains interest in endless runners.** The genre's
   retention playbook is "introduce a progression system that rewards continued play — unlocking
   new characters, power-ups, or environments" and "a variety of environments and customization
   options to maintain player interest over time."
   *Implication:* with the economy now deeper (point above), an aspirational premium World gives
   players a concrete thing to save toward. This is what shipped this pass.
   Sources: [Endless Runner Games — Core Mechanics & Top Titles](https://logicsimplified.com/newgames/endless-runner-games-core-mechanics-and-top-titles/),
   [Endless Runner Games: Top Picks and Tips for 2025](https://ejaw.net/what-is-an-endless-runner-game/)
